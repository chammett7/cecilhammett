Coaching

For as long as I can remember I have loved tumbling. Formally, I have been an acrobat for over seventeen years and I do not plan on stopping anytime soon. As a competitive gymnast and cheerleader, I learned from some of the best athletes in the field. I now work as a traveling gymnastics coach with Activated Group. Through this company I have the incredible opportunity to travel to various primary schools in the Brisbane area and teach entry level gymnastics to the students during their physical activity hour.

Yoga

Growing up I lead a very active lifestyle. High-intensity fitness like jumping, running, and tumbling was my life; however, that all changed when I was diagnosed with cancer at the age of sixteen. Treatment took a toll on my body completely. I saw no way of ever reaching the physical ability I once had before being sick. Then, I found yoga. Yoga was my way back into my body, and in turn, back into myself. Yoga guided me from the weakest I had ever been in my life to the strongest. That is why I chose to become a yoga instructor. As an instructor I aim to show my students that yoga truly is for everyone willing to be patient and give it their all.
