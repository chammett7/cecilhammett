About Me

I was born and raised in a town called Huntsville, located in north Alabama, USA. My father lived on a farm, so anytime I would visit his home as a child, I was always outside running through the trails or playing with the animals. I never understood how much that impacted who I was until I took a second to look back on my life. In my short twenty-one years of life, I’ve experienced many things that have pushed me to evolve, change, and mold into the person I am supposed to be; however, there is one part of me that has always been constant: my sense of adventure. It is a part of me that originated from years and years of running barefoot through the mud on a somewhat invisible path most certainly made by a deer that would later evolve to be the driving force that carries me across the globe.

Coaching

For as long as I can remember I have loved tumbling. Formally, I have been an acrobat for over seventeen years and do not plan on stopping anytime soon. Tumbling and being upside down is just a great way to exercise and have fun but also a way to learn skills like body awareness and balance which can be very beneficial in life. As a competitive gymnast and cheerleader, I learned from some of the best athletes in the field. I was also a High School Cheerleader for two years and a Captain for one. I am now a traveling school gymnastics coach with Activated Group. Through this company I have the incredible opportunity to travel to various primary schools in the Brisbane area and teach entry level gymnastics to the kids during their physical activity hour. I have a passion for this sport, and I believe teaching it to younger adolescents is the most fun and safest way.

Yoga

Growing up I lead a very active lifestyle. High-intensity fitness like tumbling, stunting, and jumping was my life; however, that all changed when I was diagnosed with pediatric cancer at the age of sixteen. Treatment took a toll on my body completely. By the end of chemo my muscles were diminished, and my endurance was nonexistent. I saw no way in ever reaching the physical ability I once had before being sick. Then, I found yoga. Yoga was my way back into my body, and in turn, back into myself. Yoga guided me from the weakest I had ever been in my life to the strongest. It taught me to be patient with my body and give it everything it needs but no more than that. It taught me to push myself, but not hurt myself. That is why I chose to become a yoga instructor. If I had to choose one goal as an instructor, it would be to show others that yoga truly is for everyone, and it can do wonders for anyone willing to be patient and give their all.

MovieDVD, a console based application in Java

MovieDVD is a console-based application created for a library system. It has two forms of login, staff and member, which determine both what is visible to the user and how the user can interact with the program. It uses an uses a binary tree to store and sort through movies via popularity, genre, and alphabetization. It also uses a binary tree to store the library members which are categorized by name, borrowed movies, and phone number.
